Who we are

Us in a spotlight

Canyon Ridge HOA is located in beautiful Alderwood Manor, Washington. Secluded but minutes from the city and resides on a ridge. We are approximately 20 minutes from Downtown Seattle.

Attractions

Just 10 minutes east are home to some of Snohomish County's most beautiful farm lands including pumpkin patch for the Fall. We are also home to one of the few Chick-fil-A in the area, just minutes away.

Education

Our school districts are within Edmonds School District, with Brier and Hilltop Elementary Schools, Alderwood Middle School and Mountlake Terrace High School.

Shopping

Located just 5 minutes west are local shopping areas such as Alderwood Shopping Mall and other large retail stores: Costco, Target, Best Buy, Lowes (Home Depot coming soon) and many more!
